04 / 14 The IP

Everyone teaches tips. We built five frameworks.

A tip is something you try and forget. A framework is something you internalise and use forever. Developed across a decade of coaching, tested on 10,000+ Indian men.

1.

The CQC Protocol - Cold Approach

Compliment → Question → Conversation. Three moves that turn cold approach from "what do I say?" into a repeatable sequence you'll run in your head before every approach for the rest of your life.

2.

The FLIRT Framework - Texting & Conversation

The five-move texting system that replaces every "what do I text back?" panic. Used by 5,000+ men currently. Most see a measurable shift in reply rates within 14 days.

3.

The DATE System - First Three Dates

The first-date architecture that separates men who get ghosted from men who get second dates. Four-part structure. Three mandatory conversations. One calibration curve for escalation.

4.

The RISHTA Framework - Building Attraction Naturally

Respect → Intrigue → Safety → Teasing → Humor & Attraction. The emotional flow system that moves any conversation - whether it started on an app, at a party, or through a family introduction - from polite to curious to open to playful to romantic. Built specifically for Indian dating dynamics, including arranged marriage meetups where you have one conversation to create genuine interest.

5.

The MATCH Framework - Compatibility Filtering

Mindset → Availability → Temperament → Communication → Habits. Attraction is not enough. MATCH is the five-filter system that tells you whether she's actually aligned with you long-term - before you waste six months finding out the hard way. It separates 'she's beautiful and I like her' from 'she's actually right for the life I'm building.'
