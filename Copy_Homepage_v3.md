## 4. FIVE PROPRIETARY FRAMEWORKS

**Eyebrow:** The IP

**Headline:**
Everyone teaches tips. We built five frameworks.

**Body:**
A tip is something you try and forget. A framework is something you internalise and use forever. The Magnetic Man Method is built on five proprietary frameworks, developed across a decade of coaching, tested on 10,000+ Indian men, and not available inside any other program. Every stage of attraction - from the first approach to long-term compatibility - has a named system behind it.

### 1. The CQC Protocol - Cold Approach
**Compliment → Question → Conversation.** Three moves that turn cold approach from "what do I say?" into a repeatable sequence you'll run in your head before every approach for the rest of your life. This is how you walk up to a woman at a café in India and start a real conversation - without scripts, without pickup lines, without freezing.

### 2. The FLIRT Framework - Texting & Conversation
**The five-move texting system** that replaces every "what do I text back?" panic. Used by 5,000+ men currently. Most see a measurable shift in reply rates within 14 days. Covers matches, follow-ups, dead conversations, and escalation to dates.

### 3. The DATE System - First Three Dates
**The first-date architecture** that separates men who get ghosted from men who get second dates. Four-part structure. Three mandatory conversations. One calibration curve for escalation. Where to take her, how to run the date, when to make a move - so a second date is automatic.

### 4. The RISHTA Framework - Building Attraction Naturally
**Respect → Intrigue → Safety → Teasing → Humor & Attraction.** The emotional flow system that moves any conversation - whether it started on an app, at a party, or through a family introduction - from polite to curious to open to playful to romantic. Built specifically for Indian dating dynamics, including arranged marriage meetups where you have one conversation to create genuine interest.

RISHTA is how you stop being "the nice guy she felt nothing for" and start being the man she actually wants to see again. It works on Hinge. It works at the Shaadi.com meetup. It works at the café after the cold approach.

### 5. The MATCH Framework - Compatibility Filtering
**Mindset → Availability → Temperament → Communication → Habits.** Attraction is not enough. Most men either chase incompatible women for months or settle for the first woman who says yes. MATCH is the five-filter system that tells you whether she's actually aligned with you long-term - before you waste six months finding out the hard way.

This is the framework men wish they'd had before the wrong relationship, the wrong engagement, or the wrong marriage. It separates "she's beautiful and I like her" from "she's actually right for the life I'm building."
